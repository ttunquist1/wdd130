Content

Home page

Rexburg is a small town with a load of collage students from around the country and the world. Many of these students and the longtime residents of Rexburg struggle to find new and exciting activities to do with their friends and family, Live Rexburg will help everyone in the community to answer the biggest question in Rexburg, ‘Where are the fun things to do?’

Whats Fun In Rexburg

Live Rexburg seeks to make finding fun things to do easy and simple. All visitors will need to do is look for what they want to do or try, and we will provide them will all the information we can about the desired activity or event. We will give them the hours of the location, address, contact information, and whatever else they may need access to in order to spend more time having fun with those around them.
